Getting Started

Joining a new map is exciting but can also be confusing, especially if you're coming from a similar style server. Use this guide to help you find your start on the Junction with the help of our wide range of player-built public farms, well-connected nether hub and helpful playerbase.


Click the Junction Logo to return home at any time

Navigating the map easily

Dynmap

Dynmap, one of our non-vanilla plugins, allows you to view the entire map from your web browser as well as showing players and their coordinates.

Click to visit Dynmap



Nether Hub

Thanks to the hard work of the community our Nether hub (accessible immediately via the portal at spawn) connects the map using tunnels that extend North, East, South and West. These tunnels branch with player builds, feel free to connect your base to the tunnel that runs closest to your base's portal.
